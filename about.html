About me

As you might already have noticed my name is Bram ten Hove. I am a 27 year old webdeveloper living in Hengelo, The Netherlands.

I'm an enthusiastic person with a passion for the internet. When I'm not working at a full service agency in Enschede, I enjoy spending time discovering new webtechnologies and trying them out on my own projects. And when I'm not doing that I like spending time with my lovely wife, drinking a glass of whisky or reading a good book.

I use my blogs to tell you about webdevelopment, security, performance, things related to Drupal or whatever I discovered on the internet.
